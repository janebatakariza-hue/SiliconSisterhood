Origin of Faith foundation

After having passed through a life that made me get so far from God, and evaluating how much efforts it took me to gain back my trust for God .I felt the urge to help others passing through different situation that makes them depart from God get to know the real truth that all they need is Jesus in their lives!

About Faith foundation

Mainly Faith foundation is a foundation that was built so as to keep increasing people who get salvation through helping them in their salvation journey,providing bible verses and interacting with people by making them understand the need to have Jesus in their lives !The impact is what excites us most.
